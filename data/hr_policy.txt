AI PLANET – EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK EXCERPTS
=======================================

WORK HOURS & FLEXIBILITY
AI Planet follows a hybrid work model. Employees are expected to be available during core hours of 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM IST on working days. Outside of these hours, flexible scheduling is encouraged. Remote work is fully supported for most roles, with occasional in-person collaboration expected for team sprints or planning sessions.

LEAVE POLICY
Full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, in addition to 10 public holidays. Sick leave is provided separately at 10 days per year. Unused annual leave of up to 10 days may be carried forward to the next calendar year. Leave requests should be submitted via the internal HR portal at least 3 working days in advance for planned leaves.

PROBATION PERIOD
All new hires undergo a probation period of 3 months. During this period, performance is reviewed monthly. Confirmation of employment is subject to satisfactory performance assessment and mutual agreement at the end of probation.

CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees are expected to maintain professional behaviour in interactions with colleagues, clients, and the broader AI Planet community. Harassment, discrimination, or any form of misconduct will not be tolerated. Any concerns should be raised with the HR team directly and will be handled with strict confidentiality.

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
AI Planet conducts bi-annual performance reviews — once at mid-year (July) and once at year-end (December). Reviews assess contributions to team goals, individual growth, collaboration, and alignment with company values. Feedback is two-way — employees are encouraged to share upward feedback during the review cycle.

REIMBURSEMENTS & EXPENSES
Work-related expenses such as travel, equipment, or software subscriptions may be reimbursed upon submission of receipts and manager approval through the finance portal. Reimbursements are typically processed within 10 working days from approval.

LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT
AI Planet allocates a learning budget of INR 15,000 per employee per year for courses, certifications, or conference attendance. Employees are encouraged to pursue learning relevant to their roles and to share knowledge internally via fortnightly team sessions.

INTERNAL TRANSFERS
Employees who have completed at least 12 months in their current role may apply for internal transfers to other teams or functions. Transfer decisions are made collaboratively between HR, current team lead, and the target team lead.
